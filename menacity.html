Menacity

Branding Identity

We partnered with Menacity Clothing to develop a branding identity that would elevate their presence. The goal was to create a clean, purposeful, and emotionally resonant brand experience that connects deeply with their target audience.

Client: Menacity Clothing
Scope: Full Branding Identity
Industry: Streetwear Fashion
Timeline: Current

Menacity

Brand Vision

Menacity Clothing approached us with the vision of building a modern streetwear brand with a clear identity and a strong digital presence. We created a unified visual style and brand voice that reflects their aesthetic and connects directly with their community.

Menacity

Business Results

Within the first five months, Menacity generated over $62,000 in sales through segmented email and SMS marketing. Their redesigned Shopify website has since attracted more than 150,000 user sessions, strengthening both visibility and conversion.

Menacity

Social Growth

Through strategic social content and paid advertising, Menacity grew a loyal community of more than 60,000 followers. The brand also gained recognition from notable figures who chose to wear Menacity apparel.

Menacity

Brand System

We developed a complete brand identity system including logo direction, visuals, garment graphics, and campaign collateral. This visual foundation was built to feel modern, intentional, and rooted in streetwear culture.

Menacity

Content & Campaigns

We directed content production for social platforms and managed segmented email and SMS campaigns. Paid media execution helped expand reach, improve conversion, and sustain growth.
